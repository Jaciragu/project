My Projects

Designed an ML model for Autism Spectrum Disorder

I am proud to have designed a machine learning (ML) model focused on Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), a project that combines my passion for technology with a strong desire to make a positive impact. This project involved extensive research and data analysis to develop an innovative solution that can aid in the early detection and intervention of ASD. By leveraging advanced algorithms and cutting-edge technology, my model aims to provide more accurate and timely diagnoses.

Driver Drowsiness Detector

I am proud to share that I developed an innovative Internet of Things (IoT) model for a Driver Drowsiness Detector. This project combines my expertise in technology with a commitment to enhancing road safety. By integrating sensors and real-time data processing, the model monitors driver alertness and detects signs of drowsiness. The system then alerts the driver, reducing the risk of accidents caused by fatigue.

Safety Helmet for Coal Miners

I am proud to share that I developed an innovative IoT model of a safety helmet for coal miners, combining cutting-edge technology with a critical focus on safety. This project integrates sensors and wireless communication to monitor environmental conditions such as gas levels, temperature, and humidity in real-time. The helmet alerts miners to potential hazards, ensuring timely evacuations and enhancing overall safety.
